#  Unmute

> Speak freely. Stay anonymous. Be heard.

##  Overview
**Unmute** is an anonymous social platform that allows users to share opinions on various topics without the need for login or identity verification.  
The goal is to create a space where people can express thoughts freely without fear of judgment, bias, or social pressure.

---

## 🚀 Features

-  **No Authentication Required**  
  No login, no signup — instant access.

-  **Anonymous Posting**  
  Share thoughts without revealing identity.

-  **Topic-Based Discussions**  
  Explore categories like:
  - Technology
  - Politics
  - Education
  - Entertainment
  - and more

-  **Interact with Posts**  
  - Like / Dislike opinions  
  - Reply to discussions  

-  **Real-Time Engagement**  
  Users can instantly view and respond to opinions.

-  **Minimal Data Storage**  
  Focus on privacy and simplicity.

---

##  Purpose

Social media today often filters opinions through identity, followers, and social pressure.  
**Unmute removes those barriers**, allowing people to:

- Express honest opinions  
- Explore diverse perspectives  
- Engage in open discussions
